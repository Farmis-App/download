How to install

  1. 1

    Download

    Tap the green Download APK button above. The file will save to your phone.

  2. 2

    Allow unknown sources

    When your phone asks, tap Settings and turn on Allow from this source (also called “Install unknown apps”).

  3. 3

    Install

    Tap Install, then Open when finished. The FARMIS icon will appear on your home screen.

Google Play Protect may show a warning — this is normal for apps installed outside the Play Store. Tap Install anyway.
