## FARMIS v1.1.0

This is the biggest FARMIS update since launch. v1.1.0 adds a brand new **Healthy Diets, WASH & Soil Health** module, brings soil and nutrient information directly into crop details, and lets farmers edit their own answers in the community forum.

v1.1.0 installs over v1.0.5 in place — no uninstall required.

### What's new

- **Healthy Diets, WASH & Soil Health module.** A new section covering nutrition, water & sanitation, and soil health — accessible from the home screen, with redesigned cards, share buttons, and a cleaner grid layout.
- **Soil & nutrient profiles in crop details.** Every crop now shows its soil and nutrient profile alongside growing info, and the extension officer dashboard surfaces these as quick resources.
- **Edit forum answers.** You can now edit answers you've posted in the community forum, with improved keyboard handling and direct navigation into your diagnosis history.
- **Refreshed nutrition & soil screens.** Overhauled UI across the new modules with tighter spacing, better typography, and consistent component styling.

### Changed

- **Multi-language picker removed.** The in-app LanguagePicker has been removed across all health modules to simplify the experience. Tok Pisin and English content will be reintroduced through a different mechanism in a future release.
- **Pest detail polish.** Accordion icons are larger and the corner radius is tighter for better visual consistency.

### How to upgrade

1. Download `farmis.apk` from this page.
2. Tap the file to install — it will upgrade your existing FARMIS app in place.
3. Open FARMIS and continue as before.

Full install instructions, including Tok Pisin walkthrough: https://farmis-app.github.io/download/
